INTRODUCTION                                    *flash*

Mappings and autocmd's to help see focused areas of the screen quickly.

When switching windows, Flash() is called.

                                                *flash-<leader> @*
<leader>f                      Make the area under cursor flash

ABOUT                                           *flash-about*

Grab the latest version or report a bug on GitHub:

https://github.com/jonbri/vim-flash
